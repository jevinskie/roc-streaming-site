34 //! RTCP communicator.
35 //!
36 //! Implements bidirectional exchange of RTCP packets with other participants
37 //! of a single RTP session.
38 //!
39 //! Holds a reference to IParticipant interface, which is implemented by
40 //! sender or receiver pipeline.
41 //!
42 //! Features:
43 //!
44 //! - processes received RTCP packets, extract reports from packets,
45 //! and notifies IParticipant with reports from remote side
46 //!
47 //! - queries IParticipant with up-to-date reports from local
48 //! side, and generates RTCP packets to be sent to remote side
49 //!
50 //! For more details about streams and reports, @see IParticipant.
51 //!
52 //! This is top-level class of roc_rtcp module, glueing together other components:
53 //! - rtcp::Traverser, to iterate through blocks of compound RTCP packets
54 //! - rtcp::Builder, to construct compound RTCP packets
55 //! - rtcp::Reporter, to maintain hash table of active streams, process and generate
56 //! individual blocks of compound packets, and interact with IParticipant
